# AyahGuide · Instagram Intro Design

## Style Prompt
Reverent, cinematic stillness. Deep navy and warm gold — the palette of old manuscripts and candlelit study. Motion is deliberate and slow: things arrive with weight and leave with grace. Nothing is rushed. The viewer should feel they are entering contemplative space.

## Colors
- `#0f172a` — Navy. Background. The dark of deep reflection.
- `#f5f1e8` — Cream. Primary text. Warm, not harsh white.
- `rgba(201, 168, 76, 0.85)` — Gold. Arabic text, accents, dividers.
- `rgba(201, 168, 76, 0.5)` — Gold muted. Secondary references, subtle labels.
- `rgba(245, 241, 232, 0.65)` — Cream muted. Taglines, subdued UI text.

## Typography
- **Amiri** — Arabic Quranic text. Large, breathing line-height (2.2+). Direction RTL.
- **Playfair Display** — Display headings. Brand title, translation text.
- **Inter** — UI labels, references, URLs. Light weight, tracked out.

## What NOT to Do
- No blue, green, red, or purple. Strict navy + gold palette only.
- No exclamation points or emphatic punctuation.
- No fast or bouncy transitions. Calm energy only — blur crossfade, `sine.inOut`.
- No sharp mechanical movement. No elastic overshoot.
- No hardcoded white (#fff) — always use cream (#f5f1e8).
- No `<br>` tags in running text — let CSS max-width handle wrapping.
