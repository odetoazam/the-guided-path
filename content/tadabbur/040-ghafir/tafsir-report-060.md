## 40:60

### Ibn Kathir (English)

The Command to call upon Allah By His grace and kindness,
Allah encourages His servants to call upon Him, and He guarantees to respond. Sufyan Ath-Thawri used to say: "O You Who love most those who ask of You, and O You Who hate most those who do not ask of You, and there is no one like that apart from You, O Lord."
